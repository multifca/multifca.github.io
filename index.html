Cranberry Bean Salad

  1. Sort and rinse cranberry beans. Place in a pot with water over medium-high heat.
  2. Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until beans are tender, about 1 hour.
  3. Make sure beans are covered with water during the entire cooking process. Drain and set aside.
  4. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, parsley, dill, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Add cucumber, red onion, and tomatoes; stir to combine.
  6. Gently fold in cooked cranberry beans. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
